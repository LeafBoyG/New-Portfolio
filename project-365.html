Project 365

One photo, every day, for a year. A study in discipline and observation.

The goal wasn't to capture a masterpiece every day, but to force myself to see the world differently. Some days were grand landscapes; others were just the light hitting a coffee cup. This project taught me that consistency matters more than inspiration.
