Brand Identity & Package Design

The Problem

Start by explaining the brief. What was the goal? Who was the client? What problem were you trying to solve? (e.g., "A new local coffee shop needed a complete brand identity to stand out in a crowded market.")

The Process

Walk them through your steps. This is where you show your thinking. Mention research, mood boards, sketches, and different versions.

Continue your story. (e.g., "After exploring several typographic directions, we landed on a custom script logo to give a personal, hand-crafted feel. We paired this with a secondary sans-serif font for clarity on menus and packaging.")

The Solution

Showcase the final result. Explain *why* it works and how it solved the problem from the brief. This is where you put your best, most polished images (like mockups).
