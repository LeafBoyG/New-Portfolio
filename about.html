About Me

Hello! I'm Alfred. I am a Web Designer and Photographer fascinated by contrast—whether it’s in a black-and-white photo or a bold user interface.

I believe that great design is about storytelling. I love the discipline of coding mixed with the freedom of visual art. Whether I'm fine-tuning a layout in Figma, writing CSS animations, or waiting for the perfect light behind my camera, my goal is always the same: to create things that give pause and provoke an emotional response.

Beyond just making things look good, I want to make them work well. I am currently seeking a role where I can apply my skills in HTML, CSS, and UI Design to solve real problems, ensuring every pixel serves a purpose.
