The Backstory

Hello! I'm Alfred. I started my journey with a camera in one hand and a code editor in the other. I've always been fascinated by contrast—whether it’s the stark interplay of light and shadow in a black-and-white photograph, or the logic of JavaScript meeting the fluid creativity of CSS.

I believe that great design isn't just about how things look—it's about how they feel. Whether I'm fine-tuning a layout in Figma, building a Flask application, or grading film footage, my goal is always the same: to create work that gives people pause.

Currently, I am seeking a role where I can combine these disciplines to solve real problems for real people.
